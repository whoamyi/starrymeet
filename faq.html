What is StarryMeet?+

StarryMeet is a premium platform connecting fans with verified celebrities for one-on-one meetings. We offer a safe, professional way to meet your icons in person through three meeting types: Quick Meet (15 min), Standard Session (30 min), and Premium Experience (60 min).

How do I know celebrities are real?+

Every celebrity undergoes rigorous verification. We verify their identity with government IDs, work directly with their management teams, and ensure all profiles are authentic. Look for the "‚úì Verified" badge on celebrity profiles. We guarantee 100% authentic experiences.

Is StarryMeet available worldwide?+

Yes! We operate in 50+ cities across North America, Europe, Asia, and Australia. Celebrities travel frequently, so new locations are added regularly. Check the Browse page to see which celebrities are coming to your city.

How do I book a meeting?+

Browse celebrities, select one you want to meet, choose your preferred date/time and meeting type, fill out an application explaining why you want to meet them, and complete secure payment. You'll receive confirmation within 24-48 hours after celebrity approval.

What payment methods do you accept?+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) and debit cards. All payments are processed securely through Stripe with bank-level encryption.

When will my card be charged?+

Your card is charged immediately upon booking. If your meeting isn't approved by the celebrity, you'll receive a full refund within 5-7 business days.

What happens during a meeting?+

Meetings take place in premium, private venues. You'll have the celebrity's full attention for the duration. You can ask questions, take photos, get autographs, and have meaningful conversations. Professional and respectful behavior is expected.

Can I bring someone with me?+

Quick Meets are typically one-on-one. For Standard and Premium sessions, you may request to bring one guest‚Äîthis requires celebrity approval and may incur an additional fee. Minors must be accompanied by a parent/guardian.

Are photos and videos allowed?+

Yes! Personal photos and videos are encouraged. Bring your smartphone or camera. Premium Experiences include professional photography. Photos/videos cannot be used commercially without permission.

What is your cancellation policy?+

Full refund if cancelled 7+ days before your meeting. 50% refund if cancelled 3-6 days before. No refund for cancellations less than 3 days before. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Can I reschedule my meeting?+

Yes! You can reschedule up to 7 days before at no cost, subject to availability. For changes within 7 days, contact support. We'll do our best to accommodate you.

What if the celebrity cancels?+

If a celebrity cancels, you receive a full refund within 5-7 business days. We'll also offer priority rebooking or credits toward another celebrity meeting. Celebrity cancellations are rare.

How do I apply as a celebrity?+

Email apply@starrymeet.com with your name, social media links, and management contact. We'll guide you through verification and onboarding. The process typically takes 1-2 weeks.

How much can I earn as a celebrity?+

Earnings vary based on your pricing and availability. Top celebrities earn $50,000-$200,000+ monthly. You set your own prices and approve all bookings. Use our earnings calculator on the For Celebrities page.

How do celebrities get paid?+

Payments are processed within 7 days after each meeting via direct deposit or wire transfer. We handle all tax documentation and provide detailed earning reports. StarryMeet takes a 20% platform fee.
