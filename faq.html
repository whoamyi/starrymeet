How do I book a meeting? +

Browse celebrities, click "Book now", choose your meeting type and time, complete the application form, and submit payment. You'll receive approval within 48 hours.

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance for popular celebrities. However, last-minute slots are sometimes available—check their calendar regularly.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely.

What's your cancellation policy? +

Full refund if you cancel 7+ days before. 50% refund for 3-6 days notice. No refund for cancellations within 3 days. All refunds processed in 5-7 business days.

Where do meetings take place? +

Premium hotels or private event spaces in city centers. You'll receive venue details 24 hours before your meeting. Free parking or transit info provided.

Can I take photos and videos? +

Yes! Personal photos and videos are encouraged. Premium experiences include professional photography. Commercial use requires celebrity permission.

Can I bring a guest? +

Quick Meets are one-on-one. For Standard and Premium sessions, you can request one guest—subject to celebrity approval and additional fees. Minors must have a guardian present.

What if the celebrity cancels? +

You'll receive a full refund within 5-7 business days. We'll also offer priority rebooking or credits toward another celebrity meeting.

How do I reschedule my meeting? +

Log into your dashboard and click "Reschedule" on your booking. Free if done 7+ days in advance. For changes within 7 days, contact support.

How do I know celebrities are authentic? +

Every celebrity goes through rigorous verification. We work directly with management teams and verify identities. Look for the "✓ Verified" badge on profiles.

What's included in each meeting type? +

Quick Meet (15 min): Brief conversation, 1-2 photos, autographs. Standard (30 min): Extended Q&A, multiple photos, autographs. Premium (60 min): In-depth discussion, mentorship, professional photos, video message, exclusive memorabilia.

What if I'm running late? +

Call our support line immediately at +1 (555) 123-4567. We'll try to accommodate you, but meetings may be shortened. Late arrivals beyond 10 minutes may result in cancellation without refund.
