Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially for popular celebrities. However, some celebrities offer last-minute availability, so check their calendar regularly. The earlier you book, the more time slots you'll have to choose from.

What if the celebrity cancels? +

If a celebrity cancels your meeting for any reason, you'll receive a full refund within 5-7 business days. We'll also offer you priority rebooking with the same celebrity or credits toward a meeting with another celebrity. While cancellations are rare, your satisfaction is our priority.

Can I reschedule my meeting? +

Yes! You can reschedule up to 7 days before your meeting at no additional cost, subject to the celebrity's availability. To reschedule, log into your dashboard and click "Reschedule" on your booking. For changes within 7 days of your meeting, contact our support team for assistance.

What's included in each meeting type? +

Quick Meet (15 min): Brief conversation, 1-2 photos, and autographs. Standard Session (30 min): Extended conversation, Q&A, multiple photos, and autographs. Premium Experience (60 min): In-depth discussion, mentorship, professional photoshoot, video message, and exclusive memorabilia.

How do I know meetings are authentic? +

Every celebrity on StarryMeet goes through a rigorous verification process. We verify their identity, work directly with their management teams, and ensure all meetings are legitimate. You'll see a "‚úì Verified" badge on authentic celebrity profiles. We guarantee 100% authentic experiences or your money back.

What's the cancellation policy? +

Full refund if you cancel 7+ days before your meeting. 50% refund if you cancel 3-6 days before. No refund for cancellations less than 3 days before the meeting. All refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. We recommend purchasing travel insurance if you're booking travel for your meeting.

Can I bring a friend or family member? +

Quick Meets are typically one-on-one. For Standard and Premium sessions, you can request to bring one guest‚Äîthis is subject to celebrity approval and may incur an additional fee. Please mention this in your booking application. Children under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.

Are photos and videos allowed? +

Yes! Personal photos and videos are encouraged. You can use your smartphone or camera to capture the moment. For Premium Experiences, we offer professional photography services. However, you cannot use photos/videos for commercial purposes without written permission from the celebrity.

How do I get to the venue? +

Detailed venue information and directions are sent via email 24 hours before your meeting. Venues are typically located in premium hotels or private event spaces in city centers. Free parking or public transportation information is provided. If you need assistance, our concierge team is available 24/7.

What if I'm running late? +

Please arrive 15 minutes early for check-in. If you're running late, call our support line immediately at +1 (555) 123-4567. We'll do our best to accommodate you, but meetings may need to be shortened to respect the celebrity's schedule. Late arrivals beyond 10 minutes may result in cancellation without refund.
