Product & Engineering 📍 San Francisco, CA or Remote 💼 Full-time

  • 5+ years of product management experience, preferably in marketplace or creator economy platforms
  • Track record of shipping products that drove significant user growth and engagement
  • Strong data-driven decision making and analytical skills
  • Experience working with cross-functional teams and managing stakeholder expectations
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to influence without authority

Trust & Safety Specialist

Trust & Safety 📍 Los Angeles, CA 💼 Full-time

Protect our community by developing and enforcing safety protocols, conducting background checks, and responding to security incidents. You'll ensure every meeting between fans and celebrities is safe, secure, and professional.

Requirements:

  • 3+ years in trust & safety, risk management, or security operations
  • Experience with background checks, identity verification, and fraud prevention
  • Strong judgment and decision-making skills under pressure
  • Knowledge of privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA) and platform safety best practices
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

  • Deep understanding of the entertainment industry and celebrity brand management

  • Willingness to travel and attend industry events

Senior Full Stack Engineer

Engineering 📍 Remote (US) 💼 Full-time
